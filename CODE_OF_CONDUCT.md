# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

In the interest of fostering an open and welcoming environment, we as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Be kind, respectful, and courteous
- Use inclusive language
- Assume best intent
- Be open to constructive feedback
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Offensive comments (including regarding gender, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, disability)
- Posting other people’s private information without explicit permission
- Trolling, insulting, or abusive behavior
- Public or private harassment of any contributor

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for enforcing this code of conduct. Reports should be made to `maintainer@sports-prediction-system.local`.

If you are contacted by a maintainer regarding a potential violation, please cooperate with investigators.

## Reporting

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at [maintainer@sports-prediction-system.local](mailto:maintainer@sports-prediction-system.local).

All reports will be kept confidential.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies both within project spaces and in public spaces when an individual is representing the project.
