## ❓ FAQ

**Q: Is donating required?**  
A: No. This project is 100% open-source and free to use. Donations are purely voluntary.

**Q: Are donations tax-deductible?**  
A: Consult your accountant. Cryptocurrency donations may have tax implications depending on your jurisdiction.

**Q: What if I can't donate?**  
A: No problem! Ways to contribute without money:

- Star the repo
- Share your experience using Candle Patterns
- Report bugs or suggest features
- Help other users in discussions

**Q: Can I donate other cryptocurrencies?**  
A: Currently accepting Bitcoin, Ethereum, and USDT. For other options, [open an issue](https://github.com/Zed-777/candle-patterns/issues).

**Q: Will you prioritize feature requests from donors?**  
A: Roadmap is managed through [MPDP.md](MPDP.md) and GitHub issues. Donations don't influence priority (merit-based). However, your feedback is always valued regardless of support.

**Q: How do you use donations?**  
A: Transparently — see "What Your Support Funds" above. Development time is the primary cost.
